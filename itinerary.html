Our Programme

Depart for New Delhi on Friday, 27th March 2026

India Day 1: Sat, March 28th, 2026

Overnight in Delhi

Arrive in Delhi late Friday night or early Saturday morning. After lunch, visit the Sai Baba Temple and the Our Lady of Vailankanni Shrine (Our Lady of Good Health Church). Spend some time shopping at Khan Market, then proceed to the Patte Shah Dargah in the evening. Enjoy an early dinner and overnight stay in Delhi.

India Day 2: Sun, March 29th, 2026

Overnight in Trimbakeshwar

Wake up at 3:30 a.m. and depart for the airport by 4:00 a.m. for the 7:30 a.m. flight. Arrive in Nashik at 9:35 a.m. and visit the Kala Ram Mandir in Panchvati en-route. Reach the Trimbakeshwar hotel by 1:00 p.m. After lunch, visit the Trimbakeshwar Temple. Enjoy an early dinner in the evening. Overnight stay in Trimbakeshwar.

India Day 3: Mon, March 30th, 2026

Overnight in Shirdi

In the morning wake-up 4:45am visit Trimbekeshwar temple at 5:30am. After breakfast depart for Shirdi at 8:30am in the morning. Lunch on route. Arrive at the Shirdi hotel by 1:30pm. Visit Sai Baba temple in the evening. After dinner overnight in Shirdi.

India Day 4: Tue, March 31th, 2026

Overnight in Delhi

Wake-up 4:00am, visit Sai temple in the morning at 4:30am, checkout at 11:30, lunch on route, then depart for the airport at 1:30pm. Arrive in Delhi at 6:30pm. Check into Delhi hotel at 8:30pm. After dinner, overnight in Delhi.

India Day 5: Wed, April 1st, 2026

Overnight in Guwahati

Wake-up 6:00am, breakfast at 6:30am depart for the airport at 7:30am. Flight departure at 10:20am arrive in Guwahati at 12:45pm. Check-in at 2:30. Immediately freshen and visit Maa Kamakhya Devi temple at 3:30pm. After dinner, overnight in Guwahati.

India Day 6: Thu, April 2nd, 2026

Overnight in Delhi

At 5:00 am in the morning visit Maa Kamakhya Devi temple. After breakfast check-out at 10:30am. Flight departure at 1:15pm arrive in Delhi at 3:55pm. Check into Delhi hotel at 6:00pm. After dinner, overnight in Delhi.

India Day 7: Fri, April 3rd, 2026

Overnight in Delhi

In the morning, we will go to Vrindavan and visit Neem Karoli Baba Samadhi, Devraha Baba Samadhi and Sripad Baba Ashram. In Mathura we will visit Krishan Janambhoomi temple. After dinner, overnight in Delhi.

India Day 8: Sat, April 4th, 2026

Overnight in Delhi

After breakfast, check-out at noon. After lunch meet Master Shashi. Depart in the evening/ night for the airport as per flight schedules.

India Day 9: Sun, November 1st, 2025

Delhi

Depart for the airport as per flight schedules.
